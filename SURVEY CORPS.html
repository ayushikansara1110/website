    • Who are we?

    The Survey Corps is the branch of the military most actively involved in direct Titan combat, Titan study, human territory expansion and outside-the-walls exploration. It consists of the best soldiers who are the most skilled in using Omni-Directional Mobility Gear, more commonly known as ODM Gear. We at the Survey Corps symbolize the “hope of mankind” with our insignia being known as the “wings of freedom.”

    Someday, our efforts and sacrifices will change the world and we will be able to recover what was taken away from humanity.

    What do we do?

    The Survey Corps was previously in charge of the exploration and the eventual reclamation of human territory from Titan infested lands through the act of setting up small bases, camps and resupply stations. After the fall of Wall Maria, the Corps began laying down supplies from Trost district to Shiganshina district in preparation to seal the breach and reclaim Wall Maria.

    Over the years, the corps have unearthed a goldmine worth of information about the titans and housed titan shifters. The Corps is responsible for uncovering the origin of titans and informing the common citizens whose memories were erased.
